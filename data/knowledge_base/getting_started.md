# ProTaskFlow - Getting Started Guide

Welcome to **ProTaskFlow**, your all-in-one project management and team collaboration platform. This guide will help you get set up quickly.

## Quick Start

### 1. Create Your Account
1. Visit [app.protaskflow.com](https://app.protaskflow.com)
2. Click "Sign Up" and enter your email
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile setup

### 2. Create Your First Workspace
A workspace is your team's home base. To create one:
1. Click the **+** button in the sidebar
2. Select "New Workspace"
3. Name your workspace (e.g., "Marketing Team")
4. Invite team members via email

### 3. Create Your First Project
1. Inside your workspace, click "New Project"
2. Choose a template or start from scratch
3. Add project details (name, description, due date)
4. Assign a project owner

### 4. Add Tasks
1. Open your project
2. Click "Add Task" or press `T`
3. Fill in task details
4. Assign to team members
5. Set priority and due date
