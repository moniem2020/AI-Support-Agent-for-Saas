## Billing FAQ

### How do I upgrade my plan?
1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm upgrade

Your new plan takes effect immediately, and you'll be charged a prorated amount.

### How do I add or remove users?
1. Go to **Settings** > **Team Members**
2. Click "Add Member" or select a member to remove
3. Billing adjusts automatically at the start of the next billing cycle

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Business/Enterprise only)
- Annual invoicing (Business/Enterprise only)

### How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Complete the feedback survey
4. Confirm cancellation

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation.

### Can I get a refund?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. Contact support@protaskflow.com for refund requests.

### How do I download invoices?
1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click "Billing History"
3. Click the download icon next to any invoice

### Is there an annual discount?
Yes! Pay annually and save 20%:
- Pro: $9.60/user/month (billed annually)
- Business: $19.20/user/month (billed annually)

## Tax Information

Prices shown exclude applicable taxes. VAT/GST is added at checkout based on your location. For tax-exempt organizations, please contact billing@protaskflow.com with your exemption certificate.
